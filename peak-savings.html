Peak Savings - UI/UX Design

Overview

Peak Savings is a money saving mobile app designed for iOS mobile devices. Functionality was the driving force behind this entire project. The user, of course, was also at the forefront of the design process. For user flows, wireframing, user testing, mockups and more to get feel for the whole project, please click the button below where you can see the entire case study.

Sample Mockups

The screens below are just a sampling of a few the screens found within the app.

Users are greeted with a 'Splash' screen that quickly transitions into a 'Log in' screen where the user can log in or choose to continue on to a 'Sign up' screen to create an account.

The 'Home' screen welcomes the user and immediately gives them a choice to create a new goal or continue with a current goal.

When selecting a current goal, the user can see a quick overview of their progress with the associated graphic.

The 'My Money' screen allows the user to view their money spent or saved or spent and take a look at a quick overview on the graph sitting on the top half of the screen.

Although the user can link accounts, there are times that person may make a purchase with a different payment method, such as cash, and would like to make note of it in the app. They can enter that information on the 'New Spending' screen.

On the 'Settings' screen, the user can quickly make adjustments to their profile, but most importantly they can add and remove both linked financial accounts and current goals in an economic manner.
