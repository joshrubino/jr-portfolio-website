About

My Story

My journey as a creative professional is ever evolving and in constant motion. This creative career began as a Photographer. As my interests expanded and my knowledge increased, I transitioned to Graphic Design. Since that move to Graphic Design, my curiosity has only grown, and I have now taken those skills into UI/UX Design. It's fun to be able to move seamlessly from one skill to the next and see how each skill can inform another and make my deliverables even stronger. I don't intend on keeping my feet flat on the ground. I enjoy each of these skills and I am excited to continue to evolve while I keep moving forward. Away from work, my family and I like to spend as much time outdoors as we can. I enjoy riding trails on my mountain bike, hiking, running, basketball and baseball. Staying active and laughing while doing so makes life a lot more enjoyable.
